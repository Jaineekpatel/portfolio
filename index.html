What is Home schooling?

Homeschooling is an educational choice in India, wherein parents take charge of their child’s learning from the comfort of their homes.

This means that the child is not enrolled in a physical school. But continues with his/her education at home under parental guidance, it doesn’t mean that child will learn in a four walls of home but they also explore the practical and virtual learning of of method.
